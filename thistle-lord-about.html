Just head back the way ye came, if ye can... There's no knowing for sure whether you'll make it back through the labyrinth of my mind. But I digress... Deep in the enchanted forest, ye will find ancient projects from my days as a young school boy. Don't mind the creatures, they don't bite, usually. If ye wander into the less-deep enchanted forest ye will find all the projects I'm currently experimenting with. There's lots of loose magic about in there but you should be fine.
