The Obligatory First Post

June 13th 2025

Well, it had to happen, didn’t it? You can’t very well start a blog without an initial post, can you? Hmm… I suppose you could skip the first post and proceed to the second, but then you might lose count along the way. Carry the seven, remainder of two- that sort of thing. Eventually you end up with an assortment of articles that make no sense whatsoever and you spend the rest of eternity playing catch up and wondering who’s in first place… It reminds me of Hilbert’s paradox of the Grand Hotel (Infinite Hotel Paradox), something we’ll discuss later, I’m sure. But enough of that. I’m supposed to be writing out this Brilliant Obligatory First Post after all. I can’t just ramble on for the next nine hundred words, or how every many are left….

I’ve just checked, and wouldn’t you know it, I was actually, very nearly, almost quite accurate with that word count! I didn’t have to carry anything heavy at all. Well, we’re down to roughly eight hundred at this point. What’s that? Oh! I suppose I should have mentioned that before now. My sincere apologies. Yes, I have decided to keep each post to roughly one thousand words as a bit of a challenge.

“What sort of challenge?” you ask warily.

Well, obviously there will be a great deal of effort involved in keeping the number that low for someone like me. I’m neurodivergent after all, and my AUDHD says “NO! YOU MUST WRITE DOWN EVERY. SINGLE. LITTLE. DETAIL. THEY MUST HAVE ALL OF THAT INFORMATION, OR HOW WILL THEY EVER KNOW WHAT YOU’RE TALKING ABOUT!” Yet another topic for discussion at a later date.

So, how many words are left now…

Well, look at that! We are past three hundred already. I suppose I really should begin writing about something proper, or you’ll leave the page to save yourself from a terrible migraine. I can just hear you now, “What is so difficult about writing something, anything at all for an introductory post?!” Hopefully you won’t stumble into paroxysm along the way. Just take a deep breath and continue reading. I promise a decent topic eventually.

Now where was I…

Oh, yes! The topic of discussion for this Incredibly Brilliant Obligatory First Post. Alright, here we go…

I’ve decided to give you all a little background into who I am and what I do…

Hmm…

Once again, I suppose it must be rather obvious from the blog title. Well, it’s true. I’m queer, and I’m a hermit. Now, I know some of you are shaking your heads and scoffing at my words. “Hermit? Surely not. No one is really a hermit, are they?”

“Yes!” I say to you. Some of us truly are hermits, for whatever reason. More on all that later as well. Anyway, I’m getting off topic again.

Some of you are saying to yourselves, “But, you said you were going to talk about being a queer hermit. Isn’t that what you’ve just begun telling us?”

Yes. I started to tell you all about that, but my prose is being hijacked by itself. I may not get it back. So, let’s see…

We are now more than five hundred words into our post, and I’ve told you two things about myself. Firstly, I’m queer. Secondly, I’m a hermit. Everyone got that bit? Good. So now we can move on to the details.

You will find that many of my posts ramble, but hopefully it’s a nice little andante-of-a-stroll through my AUDHD-riddled mind. I will have future posts about neurodiversity as well.

Hmm… I should be telling you about the types of posts I’ll make, shouldn’t I? That will help you decide whether this is the right blog for you. So where do I begin with that. There are lots of potential ways to get through this, but I think I need to take the direct approach and just hit you all over the head with it.

I will be blunt with the serious stuff and whimsical with the mundane. So, I guess that means I need to get the serious stuff out of the way first. Are you ready? Here goes…

FREE PALESTINE!!!!!

Are you still reading?

Oh, good.

We’ve gotten past the first little hurdle. If you can stomach the truth thus far, then we’ll get along just fine. So, yes. There will be a lot about social justice in this blog eventually. And make no mistake about it, much of it will be Palestine related. If you haven’t woken up to it yet, you can use this blog as a steppingstone to your future in helping to liberate the world.

Whoa! That was a bit more serious, wasn’t it? Well, we’re not finished yet. I could quite literally write for hours on end about the injustice, but I did set myself a goal on a mere one thousand words for each post, and we are rapidly coming up on that.

So, you’ve had a peek at the serious side, now let’s get back to the whimsy.

I will attempt to write a blog entry at least once a week, however, I am a very busy parent of young and highly precocious children, so yeah, we’ll see how well that works out. As a very busy parent – I spend about 99.99% of my time with them, and the other .01% is spent trying to sleep (hmm… that can’t be right. Where’s the time for blogging going to fit in? I guess I’ll need to carry a few remainders)- I put my kids first. ALWAYS. So, yeah. Future posts might be a little inconsistent, but I’ll do my best to keep up the pace.

Oh, my! We’ve nearly reached one thousand words! I told you we’d get there in the end. And you learned a tiny bit about me along the way. Well, I suppose that wraps up my Most Amazing, Incredibly Brilliant, Obligatory First Post. I hope you’ve enjoyed our little journey today. Bye!

(Damn it all! I went over the count by sixteen words! Oh, now it’s twenty-seven words! I’ll just shut up, then the number will stop climbing. But it’s still climbing, because I keep writing! ARGH!)
