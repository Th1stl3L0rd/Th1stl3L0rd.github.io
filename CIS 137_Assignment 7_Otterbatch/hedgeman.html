HedgeMan

The True Identity of Martin Freeman

This skittish creature can be found in many locations but is most often seen in his native territory of London, England. He is most commonly identified by his wide twinkly eyes, furrowed brow, cute button nose, and timid squeaking(which is especially common during epic battle scenes). These characteristics are what attract people the most and they have been known to search day and night for a chance of photographing him. His most loyal fans affectionately refer to him as "Hedgeman" or "FreeHog" when speaking of him. "Oh my, he's getting flustered!" and "He's so cute when he's giddy!" are some of the most common responses when sighted. Although he is sometimes mistaken for the equally elusive OtterMan, due to the fact that they maintain such a close friendship. Sceptics question his origins and dispute the vast collection of evidence. Although we only have a limited understanding of HedgeMan and much of the research is still inconclusive, the true believers continue to spread the word. If the photos and stories below aren't enough to convince you of the existence of HedgeMan then just remember one thing. If something quacks like a duck....

Notice the severe agle of the jaw line and flared nostrils. The gleam in the eyes is uncanny and if you stare long enough you will see the depths of Hades. At this time, it is inconclusive as to whether or not the fangs have been cosmetically altered.

The meditative stance is unmistakable. The change in eye color is thought to be mood related and the gasping is usually performed during moments of great epiphany.

Great sadness is displayed rarely but when revealed to us can have a truly devastating effect. The frumpled chin and pouting lip are only seen when well-loved soap opera characters die.
