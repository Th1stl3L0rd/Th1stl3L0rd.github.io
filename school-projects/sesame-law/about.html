About Us

Sesame Family Law is a premier divorce and family law practice serving clients throughout New York. When families in transition face challenges that cannot be resolved in the home, going to court may not be the best answer. The emotional and financial burden of a drawn-out legal battle can weigh heavily on every member of the family, but children are especially vulnerable. For this reason, it is wise to consult with an experienced family law attorney.
